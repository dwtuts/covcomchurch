Welcome To Covenant Community Church

What to expect if you visit.

We meet at the "Education Station" On the corner of Legion and Division Streets Whitehall, Montana. Communion Feast on the 2nd Sunday of the month

God-ward, Covenant Family & Joy filled

We are a vibrant body of saints who desire to live out the Biblical Christian faith by applying God's Word to every area of life. We know that the scripture speaks to every sphere of life;

  • Personal beliefs,
  • Morals and ethics;
  • Marriage and family;
  • Church and its government; and last but certainly not least;
  • the civil law and government.

Perhaps the most pressing question facing Christians is this: Am I free to live out his life in the manner that the culture instructs or am I to be ruled and regulated by the Spirit of God through His illuminating Law/Word? What are the implications here?

We desire to live in true Christian community, with a culture, churches and civil laws that honor God and declare truth to the nations. We search the Scriptures to discover what is TRUE and then LIVE accordingly. We want to THINK biblically and then ACT on what we KNOW. We look for the day when godliness, peace and harmony will be the rule in our families and communities. When all nations will be governed by truth, righteousness and the fear of God. We pray that you will be strengthened, encouraged and convicted unto godliness as you consider what you read here. As you browse these pages you will get a good deal of information about our fellowship, but just as the Gospel message invites man to "come, taste and see that the Lord is good" whatever you can glean from these pages will be like trying to taste an apple by reading about apples. If you are looking for a life of meaning and fullness, joy and prosperity in the Lord, come; sup with us.
